PC Assemble Lab

The PC assembly lab was a great experience for me, as it gave me knowledge and skills beyond the theoretical understanding. I got hands-on experience of assembling and re-assembling computers in a team which was one of the most significant gains. It is through this practical exposure that I gained deeper insight into computer hardware and improved my problem-solving abilities pertaining to connections and troubleshooting during the assembly process. From motherboards to power supplies, I have learned the inner workings of a computer in totality. I have become knowledgeable about the intricate connections and interconnectivity among different parts, which has broadened my views on technology beyond conventional classroom learning. Activities involved handling components, joining cables and fastening objects together which added to my practical skills. This physical interaction linked up with academic ideas presented during lectures hence making it easier to understand what was taught. My sincere thanks go to Dr. Azurah, as well as all other technicians who offered their guidance and assistance throughout these lab sessions.
